Thrift Store

Our Thrifty Church Mouse Thrift Store is a great part of our outreach.

Through our sales we have been able to contribute to local groups, including: St. Matthews House, The Salvation Army, Royal Palm Exceptional School, Co-op Community Center, Teen Challenge, Dress for Success, Abuse Counseling & Treatment, SMPD Outreach, Harry Chapin Food Bank, a local homeless shelter, and other organizations as the need arises.

The store, managed by church members, is open on Fridays and Saturdays from 9am to 2pm.

We have adult clothing, housewares, holiday items, office items, games, golf items, books, CD's, jewelry, and other items of interest.

Each weekend we offer 50% off different merchandise categories.

We accept good quality, useable donations.
