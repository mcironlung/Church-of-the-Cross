About

Paul and Janice Tedesco

Lead Pastor

The Tedesco's came to begin their new ministry at Church of the Cross early in 2021. They have been married for 48 years and have served together in full-time ministry (in a variety of ministry roles) all across the country...from coast to coast.

Paul grew up in Erie, PA and Janice in Miami, FL. Both of them attended college in Lakeland, FL. They have one son and as empty nesters now, they have a very active mini Schnauzer.

"I want to help you get from where you are, to where God wants you to be...a fully-committed follower of Jesus Christ" -Paul

A Brief History of Church of the Cross

Vision and Imagination inspired the purchase of 8.5 acres of land near a dirt road named Daniel's in the early 1980's. Soon after that, the Horizon Evangelical Friends Church began. Later, Horizon Church shared the property with a new church in Fort Myers, Pilgrims United Church of Christ. In 1988, the two churches entered into a partnership to build a new Worship Center and Chapel where each congregation could meet for services. Sharing a common bond of a love for Christ and for His work, their vision grew, and the merger of Horizon Evangelical Friends Church, and the Pilgrims United Church of Christ became a reality forming the new Church of the Cross.

On July 1, 2003, the first Church of the Cross worship service was held. In July 2007, the new Community Christian Center (CCC) was completed in order to provide a place for worship, culture, and recreation to the surrounding community. Church of the Cross continues to reach out to our community and to be committed to ministering the Word of God and the love of Jesus Christ to all who come.

Our Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible to be the inspired, the only infallible, authoritative Word of God.

God

We believe that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons; Father, Son and Holy Spirit. 

Jesus

We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His vicarious and atoning death through His shed blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and in His personal return in power and glory.

Salvation

We believe that for salvation of lost and sinful people, regeneration by the Holy Spirt is absolutely essential. We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life.

Resurrection

We believe in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they that are saved unto the resurrection of life and they that are lost unto the resurrection of damnation.

Unity

We believe in the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.
