Ministries

Adult Bible Study Class

We meet Sunday from 8:45 am - 9:30 am in our Chapel.

Tuesday Morning Prayer Breakfast

We meet in the Community Christian Center at 7:45 am - 8:45 am.

Women's Bible Study & Fellowship

We meet once a month on Thursdays (usually the third Thursday of the month) at 11 am in various homes from October thru May. See calendar for the next event.

Women's Day Out

The Women's Study and Fellowship is off during the summer, but we have a lunch gathering once a month in its place at various restaurants in the area. See calendar for the next event.

Men's Fellowship

Our "IRON MEN" group meets once a month for lunch or breakfast at various times and locations. See calendar for the next event.

B.R.E.A.D.

Building Relationships, Encouragement and Discovery

We meet in the Community Christian Center. Our meetings include:

  • Singing songs
  • Sharing info about ourselves
  • Sharing a devotional and scripture passage and discussing it
  • Praying with and for each other
  • Discussing ways to promote and grow our church
  • This meeting concludes with lunch

Worship Ministry

There are various opportunities to use your God-given gifts and talents vocally, instrumentally or in our tech/media areas.

Thrifty Church Mouse Store

Our thrift store is open on Fridays and Saturdays from 9 am - 2 pm. It is located on the back of our property. We have 50% off certain categories each week. Come in to browse our great selection.
